# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for people building self-hosted AI tools, regardless of background, experience level, identity, or location.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially with new contributors.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback when reviewing issues or pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Any behavior that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

This document is inspired by the Contributor Covenant, adapted to keep the policy short and practical for this project.
